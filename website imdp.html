Corleone mafia family in New York. The head of the family, Don Vito Corleone (played by Marlon Brando), is a wise, respected, and feared patriarch known as “The Godfather.”

named Sollozzo, leading to an assassination attempt that leaves him seriously wounded. In his absence, his hot-tempered son Sonny steps in to run the family. Meanwhile,

Sollozzo and a corrupt police officer, then escapes to Sicily. While hiding in Sicily, Michael marries a local woman, Apollonia, who is later killed in a car bomb meant for him.
